Fixture Equalisation Proposal

Teams who play each other twice will be playing for half points (2 points for a win, 1 point for a draw), points for/against will also be halved for those games when calculating percentage.

Advantages:

  • All teams play each team for the same amount of points each season creating an equal draw for everyone - maximum points possible is 68 ((12 x 4) + (10 x 2)).
  • Minimal to no advantage/disadvantage when playing the best/worst team twice (playing bottom teams should be easier games, playing top teams you have the opportunity to take points off them twice)
  • No major changes to current scheduling algorithm required
  • No floating games

Disadvantages:

  • Some games may be seen as less important as they are for less points
  • More complex to predict ladder near the end of the season when some teams have 2 points games remaining

Caveats:

  • To make a truly equal competition over for example a 4 year period all teams should have an equal number of home/away games against each opposition club
